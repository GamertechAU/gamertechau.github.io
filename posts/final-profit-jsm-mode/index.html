Final Profit: JSM Mode

by GamertechAU
published on 11 October 2024

Roguelike retail therapy

NameFinal Profit: JSM Mode
Completed On2024/10/11
DevelopersBrent Arnold
PublishersBrent Arnold

Intro

My second playthrough of Final Profit. You can read about the first run here. Manabrent released the 1.2.3 update which came with a new optional roguelike game mode, Just Shop Mode(JSM). Of course, I had to give it a go.

Plot

As the name suggests, instead of having access to everything in the game like in the base mode, you get just the Enterpriston store. The player now gains access to new products to sell and customers buying them via roguelike unlock mechanics that change the run every time. Customers only buy certain products, so you have to adjust your tactics to what's available.

Gameplay

There's configurable choices when starting the mode that affect the difficulty. Directly affecting how many products, customers and buffs you can unlock. Even picking the 'Normal' difficulty choices, the new mode was challenging. Looking at you tax-man...

Being a stripped-down game mode, JSM has minimal story however still strongly rewards exploration and progression. There's lots to find including new puzzles and secrets, and multiple additional unique endings. An entire room full of unlocks, upgrades and tweaks you can make to your customers, products and store. Plenty of customisation options to make the store your own and can still choose between generous and ruthless game styles.

Tech

Performance was just as smooth and stable as the last play through. Also still Platinum rated on ProtonDB and Steam Deck Verified.

Recommendation

Nothing's changed here. Final Profit is still awesome.

If you liked the base game mode, definitely give the JSM game mode a go.
