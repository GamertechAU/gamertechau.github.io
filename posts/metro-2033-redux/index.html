Metro 2033 Redux

published on 31 March 2023

Post-nuclear disaster with a twist

NameMetro 2033 Redux
Completed On2023/03/31
Developers4A Games
PublishersDeep Silver

Playthrough

The first in the Metro series and remastered for the Redux edition, Metro 2033 is a solid first-person shooter exploring the post nuclear war landscape of Moscow, or more accurately, the underground subway metro system built to function as bunkers in the event of a disaster.

You play as Artyom, born shortly before the last days in 2013. Now 20 years later, your home is under attack from the "dark ones" and you're forced to travel to the underground capital to request aid to protect the settlement.

As you explore the metro and scavenge unique makeshift weapons and ammo, gas mask filters to protect against the irradiated surface and very limited medical supplies, you also have to contend with mutated monsters, dark ones, human factions with their own agendas as well as the environment itself.

The stealth system is rewarding and lets you ambush enemies effectively. If you're discovered though, the game tends to throw almost every enemy in the area at your face.

The game's atmosphere and soundtrack is detailed and expresses the mood of the game accurately. Hiding from certain monsters as they pass by gets the heart rate going.

The game is pretty well made. There are a number of unfixed crash bugs, and the optimisation in some areas leaves a lot of be desired however it runs smoothly.

Highly recommend the Metro Redux series. It's an enjoyable game. Took about 15 hours on stream.
