Final Profit: A Shop RPG

published on 25 January 2024

Details

NameFinal Profit: A Shop RPG
Completed On2024/01/25
DevelopersBrent Arnold
PublishersBrent Arnold

Playthrough

Definitely a unique game which immediately grabbed my attention.

Final Profit is made by a single dev who continues to update it with new content long after release. Some people hate on games made with RPG Maker, but this game shows that's it's possible to make solid, enjoyable games with 30+ hours of gameplay.

You play as the queen of the Fae, defending her people from a capitalist corpo taking over the world using all the scummy tactics you'd expect by beating them at their own game. How exactly you accomplish that is up to you.

There's many paths and endings to choose from. You can choose to become the benevolent queen protecting your people and the innocents around you, you can also go full ruthless, destroying the lives of the people you encounter to accomplish your goals, or anywhere in between.

The majority of the content you can discover in the game is optional, but they all impact the player should you choose to complete them. Exploration is heavily rewarded. There's fun puzzles, secrets everywhere and many different styles of gaining profits and power to suit the varied gameplay styles of the playerbase, so you can build your empire how you like and all of it is good gameplay.

It was highly enjoyable to stream and engaging to chat. The developer himself even popped into the channel to talk shop and chill, so if you're streaming the game and Manabrent pops in say hi, they're a decent bloke.
