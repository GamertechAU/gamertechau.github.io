Sleeping Dogs Definitive Edition

by GamertechAU
published on 22 May 2023

One heck of a story

NameSleeping Dogs: Definitive Edition
Completed On2023/05/22
DevelopersFeral Interactive
PublishersSquare Enix

Intro

The remaster of the original 2012 game. Sleeping Dogs Definitive Edition is a extremely engaging action-adventure game.

Plot

You play as Wei Shen. An undercover Hong Kong police officer tasked with infiltrating the Sun On Yee, part of the Triad. Wei must balance his duty as an officer with his survival in the gang and many times the lines get blurred.

The main story is brilliant and well written. The game shares Wei's struggles balancing the two lives. All the while exploring the inner workings and rivalries of the Sun On Yee organisation. Will you choose to support the police? The Triad? Or perhaps another choice?

Gameplay

Explore a small segment of Hong Kong with side quests to find all over the map. Beat up gangs, catch drug dealers and investigate street racers for the police. While at the same time maintaining your cover as a criminal and pushing your way up the ranks of the Triad. Shake down civilians, destroy rivals and otherwise do things the police don't appreciate.

Combat usually involves unarmed combat, but the player also has access to guns and melee weapons. As you progress, you can also unlock heavily armed vehicles for the ultimate in destruction. You can also train with Wei's old martial arts master to improve your skills and gain new techniques.

Tech

Tech-wise the game ran well, however it does have a windowing issue on launch. If you launch in fullscreen on a modern OS it tends to fail.

Users need to edit the config to windowed mode, and launch the game. Then you can change it to fullscreen in game. Make sure you change it back to windowed before you quit out. If you're using Linux with a Plasma DE, you can lock the game to windowed. After that use a KDE window rule to force fullscreen borderless to skip the setting change every session.

The graphics quality and soundtrack are great and suit the game well.

Due to the windowing issue, Sleeping Dogs only has a Gold rating on ProtonDB and Playable on Steam Deck. If you use one of the above methods to get it working though, it runs perfectly.

Recommendation

Highly, highly recommend grabbing this game. It has excellent gameplay and an exceptionally written story. With completing most optional content, it took around 50 hours on stream.
