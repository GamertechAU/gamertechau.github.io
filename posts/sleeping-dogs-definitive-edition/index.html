Sleeping Dogs Definitive Edition

published on 22 May 2023

Details

NameSleeping Dogs: Definitive Edition
Completed On2023/05/22
DevelopersFeral Interactive
PublishersSquare Enix

Playthrough

The remaster of the original 2012 game, Sleeping Dogs Definitive Edition is a extremely engaging action-adventure game.

You play Wei Shen, an undercover Hong Kong police officer tasked with infiltrating the Sun On Yee, part of the Triad. Wei must balance his duty as an officer with his survival in the gang and many times the lines get blurred.

Explore a small segment of Hong Kong with side quest PoI's all over the map. Beat up gangs, catch drug dealers and investigate street racers for the police, all while maintaining your cover as a criminal and pushing your way up the ranks of the Triad by shaking down civilians, taking down rivals and otherwise doing things the police don't appreciate.

The main story is brilliant and well written, competently showing Wei's struggles balancing the two lives, while exploring the inner workings and rivalries of the Sun On Yee organisation.

Combat usually involves unarmed combat, but the player also has access to guns and melee weapons. Sometimes even heavily armed vehicles. You can also train with Wei's old martial arts master to improve your skills and gain new techniques.

Tech-wise the game ran well, however it does have a windowing issue on launch. If you launch in fullscreen on a modern OS it tends to fail. Need to edit the config to windowed mode, launch then you can change it to fullscreen in game. Make sure you change it back to windowed before you quit out. The graphics quality and soundtrack are great and suit the game well.

Highly, highly recommend grabbing this game. It has excellent gameplay and an exceptionally written story. With completing most optional content, it took around 50 hours on stream.
