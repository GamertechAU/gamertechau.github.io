Never Alone

published on 5 June 2023

Bring a coat

NameNever Alone
Completed On2023/06/05
DevelopersUpper One Games
PublishersE-Line Media

Playthrough

Never Alone is a charming puzzle-platformer game featuring a girl named Nuna and an arctic fox companion.

The plot revolves around the girl leaving her home to discover the source of an eternal blizzard that is destroying their home. During their travels they face dangers, solve puzzles that often require you to swap control between Nuna and the fox and see epic scenes unfold all with excellent narration.

The game sets the mood extremely well, the art and soundtrack are both spot-on and there is a lot of lore to discover during the playthrough. It will definitely get you right in the feels.

Never Alone ran perfectly with no bugs, frame drops etc. You can see the creators put a lot of effort into it.

If you're into platforming games with excellent story, I highly recommend this game. One playthrough got around 2.5 hours on stream.
