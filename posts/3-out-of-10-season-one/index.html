Playthrough

3 out of 10 is an enjoyable (also free) episodic graphic adventure game featuring a struggling game development studio, Shovelworks Studios.

Shovelworks is portrayed as the worst dev studio in the world, with games that have never broken a 3 out of 10 review rating.

You play as Midge, a brand new hire joining the studio and trying to improve the terrible (but fun) workplace environment as the game highlights significant real-life issues in game development in a fun and engaging way.

Explore the studio, a for-profit development school churning out unpaid interns, games "activists" mad at games for the sake of being mad at something and asset stores full of junk.

The game is full of mini-games within a game, providing plenty of content and lighthearted challenge to the player throughout the game. It's extremely well designed and enjoyable.

Tech-wise, the game runs perfectly. The art, soundtrack, voice actors etc are all well chosen and suit the game well.

Highly recommend playing through this series. Did I mention it's free?
