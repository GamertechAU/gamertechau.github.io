3 out of 10 Season Two

published on 23 March 2023

Completed On2023/03/23

Playthrough

3 out of 10 Season Two continues the story of Shovelworks Studios, the worst game dev studio in the world. You can find the rundown of season one here.

In Season Two, Midge has to navigate new and eccentric problems faced by the studio in five new episodes while balancing her dark secret.

As in season one, fresh new multi-genre mini-games have been included that blend well into the game's atmosphere. The player manages equipment malfunctions, suspect nightclubs and more during their playthrough.

Tech-wise, the game ran perfectly with no issues. The graphics, soundtrack and voice acting were all on point.

Highly recommend grabbing this still-free game.
