3 out of 10 Season Two

by GamertechAU
published on 23 March 2023

Part 2 of a great series

Name3 out of 10 - Season Two
Completed On2023/03/23
DevelopersTerrible Posture Games
PublishersTerrible Posture Games

Intro

3 out of 10 Season Two continues the story of Shovelworks Studios, the worst game dev studio in the world. You can find the rundown of season one here. Season one and two are both available for free via the Epic Games Store.

Plot

In Season Two, Midge has to navigate new and eccentric problems faced by the studio in five new episodes. But don't forget to keep her dark secret hidden from the rest of the studio.

Deal with hardware failures and sabotage, caffeine addictions, rogue AI, vehicular combat and more. All while trying to release a good game. There's also probably an intern around here somewhere.

Gameplay

As in season one, fresh new standalone mini-games have been included. They all blend well into the game's story and atmosphere. The player manages equipment malfunctions, suspect nightclubs and more during their play through. As well as the ever-present issues that studios face while attempting to develop a game.

Tech

Tech-wise, the game ran smoothly with no issues through the Heroic Games Launcher. The graphics, soundtrack and voice acting were all on point. No stutter, graphical corruption or optimisation issues were present.

Recommendation

Season two is a great continuation to the series. Highly recommend grabbing both free games off Epic. Looking forward to season three!
