Duck Detective: The Secret Salami

by GamertechAU
published on 20 June 2025

A quacking fun detective game

NameDuck Detective: The Secret Salami
Completed on2025-06-20
DevelopersHappy Broccoli Games
PublishersHappy Broccoli Games

Play through

Duck Detective: The Secret Salami is a simple yet fun and engaging detective story.

The main character Eugene aka the Duck Detective is a broke, freshly-divorced ex-cop. Now a private detective, he's desperate for any job to pay rent. As well as his not-so-secret bread addiction.

Luckily he does manage to land a job investigating a stolen lunch at the office of a bus depot. Fairly quickly though, he realises that there's a lot more going on here than a missing lunch.

The game map is limited to the bus office with several rooms and the surrounding area. The player explores the area interacting with marked objects and questioning characters for key information.

Once you've collected all the information, it's time to make a deducktion. By combining all the info to figure out parts of the story, the player discovers what's really going on and quacks the case!

The game ran smoothly, the audio was spot-on and the art suited the game style well. The devs provided a native Linux version which ran perfectly, though the Windows version also runs well via Proton and a has a platinum rating on ProtonDB. Greatly appreciated!

If you're into fun and relaxed detective games with a bit of humour, I highly recommend looking into the Duck Detective series. The game has around 2 hours of game play. You can find the play through of the second game in the series here.
