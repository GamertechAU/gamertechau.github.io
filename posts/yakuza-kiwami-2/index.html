Yakuza Kiwami 2

by GamertechAU
published on 10 December 2024

Always a great time

NameYakuza Kiwami 2
Completed on2024-12-10
DevelopersRyu Ga Gotoku Studio
PublishersSEGA

Intro

Yakuza Kiwami 2 is the third game in our Yakuzathon playthrough and the first in order using the new Dragon engine originally launched in Yakuza 6. It makes a huge difference to the game with lots of quality-of-life improvements over 0 & 1.

Plot

The main story is awesome, with deep serious scenes and the usual well-balanced sillyness that Ryu Ga Gotoku are known for. It's believable and fun to engage in, with plenty of time in between arcs to go explore the rest of the game. When you go back to it, you can be sure of taking content right to the face.

Gameplay

While fun, the substories (small side quests) unfortunately felt pretty shallow compared to the previous games. They have good stories, they're just over too soon and lack any sort of impact to the world aside from experience. There just wasn't enough in each to get invested. In 0 & 1, the substories got pretty deep, had characters that 'existed', in 2 once you've done the quest, that's pretty much it.

The usual Yakuza minigames are present including ofc SEGA arcade games. If you enjoy mahjong, SEGA arcades and uh...other unexpected activities, there's plenty there to play. Two major side-stories bring back some old favourite characters and integrate them well.

Tech

RGG puts time into their games and Yakuza Kiwami 2 ran perfectly out of the box. No audio, graphical or performance issues.

It's earned a Platinum rating on ProtonDB and is Steam Deck Verified.

Recommendation

Overall, really enjoyed the game and it was perfectly playable on modern hardware. The substories are a bit of a let down but they don't detract from the main game at all. If you're into the Yakuza/Like a Dragon series, don't miss this one.
