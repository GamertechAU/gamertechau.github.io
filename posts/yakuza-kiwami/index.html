Yakuza Kiwami

by GamertechAU
published on 25 October 2024

Kiryu bringing the content

NameYakuza Kiwami
Completed On2024/10/25
DevelopersRyu Ga Gotoku Studio
PublishersSEGA

Playthrough

The second in the Yakuza series. While shorter than Yakuza 0 there's no lack of content to experience. The Yakuza Kiwami remake brings freshly recorded voice lines, upgraded graphics and engine and makes full use of it.

The main story is engaging with all the twists, turns and feels you can expect from a Ryu Ga Gotoku game. As always, the game environment of Kamurochō is highly detailed and accurately modelled on its real-world counterpart of Kabukichō.

One thing the Yakuza series excels at is filling out the world with content aside from the main story. The substories are deep with characters that matter and contribute to the world, there's tons of achievements/challenges and minigames everywhere including the staple SEGA arcades. If you want to just main the story quest you'll enjoy it, however the player is surrounded by a ton of extra content that's ready to explore.

The game runs perfectly on modern hardware. There are a few minor changes that would improve it, such as removing the long pauses after finishing combat, bumping into objects and after voice interactions with NPC's. Generally taking control from the player is less than ideal, but it doesn't interfere with the gameplay itself, just a frequent annoyance.

A Yakuza game from these devs, you're going to enjoy it. It's full of enjoyable content with stories that mean something. If you're into the Yakuza series, dive on in.
