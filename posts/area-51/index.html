Area 51

published on 23 May 2023

Details

NameArea 51
Completed On2023/05/23
DevelopersMidway
PublishersMidway Home Entertainment

Playthrough

Was feeling like a bit of nostalgia, so I went back and played Midway's Area 51, with a community restoration project available here: https://lucasc.me/post/area-51-2005-windows-10-11-patches-guide

You play as Ethan Cole, a special forces operative called in to deal with an unknown situation at the infamous Area 51 blacksite. Predictably things don't go smoothly and you find yourself fighting for your life while encountering unique mechanics and dangers.

Area 51 is a first-person shooter with the player fighting their way deeper under the base, discovering secrets humanity isn't meant to know. All the while listening to great voice acting by talent such as David Duchovny, Marilyn Manson and more.

The unmodded game hasn't aged well tech-wise. It definitely needs a remaster like the one above or Project Dreamland. With the remaster, the game ran extremely well and had no issues during the playthrough.

The game was an excellent throwback and I enjoyed the playthrough. Highly recommend it. Keep in mind the ending credits song is DMCA, but the rest of the OST is stream-safe, at least on Twitch.
