Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney

published on 14 November 2024

Objection!

NamePhoenix Wright: Ace Attorney
Completed On2024/11/14
DevelopersCAPCOM
PublishersCAPCOM

Playthrough

Part one of the Phoenix Wright Trilogy, the game was a solid introduction into the series.

You play the fresh-faced criminal lawyer Phoenix Wright, defending his clients by investigating crime scenes, questioning witnesses, collecting evidence and using themÂ  in the courtroom to highlight contradictions in the prosecution's statements.

The four original episodes had enjoyable puzzles and engaging stories, believable conflict between Wright and his rival Edgey, an extremely dodgy judge and enough content that lasted for multiple streams.

The fifth episode was added in a later release of the original game and while enjoyable, suffered from some logic issues while looking for clues. The gameplay is based around finding contradictions in the prosecutions evidence (of which there's a lot) but unfortunately there were a number of obvious contradictions which apparently...weren't, such as photos showing different evidence to what's presented in court. If you get things wrong, then you lose 'points', indicating how sick the judge is of you, meaning correctly pointing out these 'fake' contradictions risks losing you the game.

Tech-wise the game ran well, with no bugs, frame drops etc.

Overall I had fun playing the game and chat enjoyed watching it and engaging with the puzzles. If you're into detective-style content like Ace Attorney then this is one to pick up.
