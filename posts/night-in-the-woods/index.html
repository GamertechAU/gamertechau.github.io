Night in the Woods

published on 16 June 2023

Details

NameNight in the Woods
Completed On2023/06/16
DevelopersInfinite Fall
PublishersFinji

Playthrough

An excellent adventure game with a great story.

The main character is Mae Borowski, a college dropout who returns to their childhood home in Possum Springs, a decaying ex-mining town going through late-stage collapse as the primary employers have left town.

Mae reunites with her parents and childhood friends and explores a changed world as well as the town's dark secret. Hang out with your friends and learn their story as well as how their lives are changing as adults. You choose which friends to hang out with during the day cycle which affects what you're able to learn during the playthrough. As you progress, you also discover the town's dark secrets.

The story likely isn't for everyone, but is extremely well written with lots of depth, optional lore and can get you right in the feels.

Tech-wise the game ran perfectly. The art design and soundtrack was well done. You shouldn't have any problems running Night in the Woods.

Overall, highly recommend this brilliant game. One playthrough got a bit over 6 hours on stream.
