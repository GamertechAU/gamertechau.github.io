Night in the Woods

by GamertechAU
published on 16 June 2023

Amazing indie story game

NameNight in the Woods
Completed On2023/06/16
DevelopersInfinite Fall
PublishersFinji

Playthrough

An excellent adventure game with a great story.

The main character is Mae Borowski, a feline college dropout. Night in the Woods starts with Mae returning to her childhood home. Arriving in Possum Springs, Mae finds a decaying ex-mining town going through late-stage collapse. The primary employers have left town and the remaining population are stuck or leaving.

Mae reunites with her parents and childhood friends to explore a changed world. as well as the town's dark secret. Hang out with your friends and learn their story as well as how their lives are changing as adults.

Time progresses as you do activities and is limited. You have to choose which friends to hang out with during the day cycle. This affects what you're able to see and learn during each play through. As you progress, you also start to uncover the town's dark secrets.

The story likely isn't for everyone, but is very well written with lots of depth and optional lore. Be prepared as it can get you right in the feels.

Tech-wise the game ran perfectly with no graphical or performance issues. The art design and soundtrack was well done. You shouldn't have any problems running Night in the Woods.

I strongly recommend this brilliant game. One play through got a bit over 6 hours on stream.
