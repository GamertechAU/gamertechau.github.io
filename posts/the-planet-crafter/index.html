The Planet Crafter

published on 24 May 2024

Details

NameThe Planet Crafter
Completed On2024/05/24
DevelopersMiju Games
PublishersMiju Games

Playthrough

In interesting planet terraformer with optional co-op that was added after my playthrough. You play as a criminal who chose to commute their sentence in return for terraforming a planet for the ruling corpos. You succeed and you're free to go. Easy, right? Just terraform a whole planet, what could go wrong?

The gameplay itself is solid. You crash land on the preset planet (a couple of choices available with DLC), collect materials and build tech to increase the terraformation level of the planet to make it habitable for humanity and profit. The game is peaceful so there's no hostiles to deal with, just the environment and lack of atmosphere. As you progress your metrics, the corpo sends you blueprints for new upgraded tech.

There's a bit of a lore-based story in the game as you learn about the planet and its history which is pretty enjoyable. Can't say much more without spoilers, but just enjoy exploring the world.

The science is...interesting as the math is slightly impractical. The planetary heat starts at 0 Kelvin, aka absolute zero where no matter can move. Even when terraforming is 'complete' the heat is at 'an electron just twitched' level. The air pressure can get pretty lethal, etc. Also when you're done you can just keep going, just keep increasing the metrics to absurd levels.

The UI is easy to understand and use. The game is stable and runs smoothly. The graphics are fairly simple but pretty. Parts of the map are lacking collision mesh so exploring a bit too wildly can clip you inside of mountains. Luckily it's easy to get back out.

This is a fun and reasonably chill game with a small but well-developed storyline. If you want to just chillax by yourself or with friends, then it's definitely a good buy.
