About the channel

The first stream on this channel was a PC building stream, building the current PC I use to stream now (PC specs here) supported by awesome streamers and friends of the channel.

The channel unlocked Twitch Affiliate on 16th January 2024. Since then, the channel has continued to grow thanks to the wonderful chatters and lurkers that lend me their time.

During an average stream, you're likely to see a large variety of games, pro-gaming skills, solid gameplay, friendly discussion, tech talk and the odd dad joke. Please be aware that while I tell dad jokes, I'm not actually a dad. I'm a faux pa.

What games do we play?

Well... Pretty much everything really. Primarily focusing on indie games over many genres including but not limited to shooters, action, beat em ups, adventure and exploration, platformers, horror, simulators, stealth, survival. The list goes on.

Light and bouncy to dark and gritty. Games are meant to be played.
